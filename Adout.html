About Udant

Udant is a digital marketplace empowering farmers to sell non-perishable goods, fostering inter-state trade. It accommodates diverse products, offers regional language support, and assists farmers in listing and pricing their goods. Logistics are initially outsourced, and physical stores act as hubs.

Through blockchain technology and smart contracts, Udant ensures secure, transparent, and cost-effective transactions. The platform's supply chain management enhances food safety, reduces waste, and improves efficiency. It connects stakeholders, allowing small farmers to access broader markets for increased income.

In summary, Udant revolutionizes agricultural trade, offering farmers market access, transparency, and efficiency through a digital platform supported by blockchain and supply chain management.
